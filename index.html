Tasty Pixel Design Process

Sketch

We start off with sketching out mock ups that match the design specification and requirements from our clients. During this process, we work closely with our customers to make sure the sketches are reviewed and approved.

Model

Once the sketches have been finalised and signed off, we begin to build out the 3D world in blender. We make sure our 3D designers are following guidelines and building out the architecture to match the required specification.

Development

Our developer that has over 15 years of experience building video games, works closely with our customers development team to support coding the final product.
